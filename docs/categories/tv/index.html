Adding a Built-in Sensor Bar to a TV

admin
Oct 7, 2010

I was thinking about moving my Wii, and relocating it to a more conspicuous place. I was annoyed at my sensor bar getting in the way: I knew that the sensor bar was just 2 IR LEDs. So why not just build those LEDs right into the TV? One less cord to lay around. Lets open it up! I used the given board that had 2 White LEDs that lit up the Westinghouse Logo.
